Scanly Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

Scanly ("the App") is a free, offline document scanner developed by Qaspro Global FZE. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. The App does not require an account, does not connect to any server, and does not transmit any information off your device.

2. Camera & Storage

The App requests access to your device camera solely to scan documents. Scanned files are saved only on your own device, in the App's private storage folder. We never upload, share, or access your scans.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google ML Kit (on-device) for document scanning and text recognition. ML Kit runs entirely on your device — no image or text leaves your phone. See Google ML Kit Terms for details.

4. No Ads, No Analytics

The App contains no advertising and no analytics SDKs. No usage data is collected or reported to any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since the App collects no personal data at all, it is safe for users of any age.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the effective date above and publish the updated version at this URL.

7. Contact

Questions? Email us at info@qasproglobal.com.
